## 9. PRODUCT CARBON FOOTPRINT (PCF) MODULE

### The Problem This Solves

A Turkish construction company (e.g., precast concrete manufacturer) wants to sell to a UK construction project. The UK buyer asks:

> *"What is the embodied carbon of your precast panels? We need ISO 14067 PCF for our BREEAM rating and our CSRD Scope 3 Category 1 reporting."*

The Turkish company has never calculated product-level carbon. They only have (maybe) an organizational GHG report. They lose the contract.

### sustain.ai PCF Module

```python
class PCFCalculation:
    """
    ISO 14067:2018 — Carbon footprint of products
    Cradle-to-gate (most common for B2B)
    """
    
    def calculate_pcf(self, product: Product, boundary: str = "cradle_to_gate") -> PCFResult:
        """
        Inputs:
        - Raw material quantities + supplier emission factors
        - Manufacturing process energy (from organizational GHG data — reused!)
        - Transport to factory gate
        - Allocation method (mass, economic, system expansion)
        
        Output:
        - PCF in kg CO2e per functional unit (per ton concrete, per m², per unit)
        - ISO 14067 compliant declaration
        - CBAM certificate data (if product is CBAM-covered)
        - EU Taxonomy alignment check
        """
        
        upstream = self.calculate_upstream_emissions(product.materials)
        manufacturing = self.allocate_manufacturing_emissions(
            product,
            organizational_ghg=self.org_ghg_data  # Reuses Module 2 data!
        )
        transport = self.calculate_transport(product.factory_location, boundary)
        
        pcf_kg_co2e_per_unit = upstream + manufacturing + transport
        
        return PCFResult(
            pcf_value=pcf_kg_co2e_per_unit,
            functional_unit=product.functional_unit,
            standard="ISO 14067:2018",
            boundary=boundary,
            cbam_eligible=product.is_cbam_covered,
            cbam_data=self.generate_cbam_data(product, pcf_kg_co2e_per_unit),
            eu_taxonomy_aligned=self.check_eu_taxonomy(product),
            declaration_pdf=self.generate_pcf_declaration(product, pcf_kg_co2e_per_unit),
        )
```
